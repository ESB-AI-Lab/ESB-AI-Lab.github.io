ESB AI Lab Corporation is a California nonprofit public benefit corporation organized exclusively for scientific, educational, and charitable purposes under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. We develop and apply computational methods in artificial intelligence, machine learning, computer vision, and genomics to address critical challenges in food security, species conservation, climate resilience, human health, science accessibility, and language preservation.

## Research Focus

1. **AI-Directed Breeding & Aquaculture:** Computer vision and genomic approaches for sustainable agriculture and aquaculture
2. **Computer Vision for Conservation:** Computational methods supporting the conservation and recovery of threatened and endangered species
3. **Climate & Disease Ecology:** Climate-driven changes in vector-borne disease distribution and vector genomics
4. **Genome Evolution & RNA Biology:** Biological adaptation to environmental change, RNA regulatory mechanisms, and structural variation
5. **Computational Pathology & Cancer Genomics:** Machine learning and genomic analysis applied to human health
6. **Endangered Language Preservation:** NLP, speech recognition, and AI-assisted OCR for endangered languages
7. **Edge AI for Field Research:** Multi-sensor edge AI systems for real-time data collection in field and farm settings
8. **Open Science & Education:** Accessible AI tools and educational programs broadening scientific participation
