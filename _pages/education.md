## Education Program

ESB AI Lab offers non-vocational educational programming in the sciences, including workshops, short courses, cohort-based intensives, research mentorships, public seminars, and open educational resources. All programs are under 32 contact hours and award certificates of completion.

### Program Areas

- Artificial intelligence and machine learning
- Data science and computational biology
- Bioinformatics and genomics
- Python programming for scientific computing
- Applied statistics for biological research

### Accessibility

ESB AI Lab is committed to broadening scientific participation. All programs include a published sliding-scale fee structure and scholarship provisions for participants from underserved communities and minority-serving institutions.
