Our paper on deep learning sex determination in a Critically Endangered species, Red Abalone, was published in Frontiers in Artificial Intelligence. This work demonstrates that convolutional neural networks can classify reproductive tissue from ultrasound imagery non-invasively, reducing stress on animals, lowering operational costs for producers, and improving scalability in breeding programs. This is the first application of deep learning to sex determination in abalone and opens a pathway for AI-assisted species assessment in aquaculture more broadly. [Read the paper](https://doi.org/10.3389/frai.2026.1794183)
