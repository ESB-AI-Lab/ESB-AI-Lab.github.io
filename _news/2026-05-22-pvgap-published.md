**PvGAP paper published in *The Journal of Infectious Diseases*.** This work describes a cost-effective amplicon panel with 80 high-diversity targets for population genomics and 8 targets of epidemiological interest for *Plasmodium vivax*, validated with Ethiopian field samples and computational assessment across three geographic regions. The panel supports drug resistance tracking, distinguishes infection origins, and helps separate reinfection from recrudescence in efficacy studies. [Read the paper](https://doi.org/10.1093/infdis/jiag270)
